# German Credit Risk Analysis

This project analyzes the German Credit dataset to identify key drivers of credit risk
and provide actionable insights for credit policy design.

## Business Questions
- Which customers are most likely to be high-risk borrowers?
- How can banks proactively reduce bad debt risk?

## Key Findings
- Loan duration is the strongest controllable risk factor.
- Very large loan amounts exhibit threshold effects.
- Business and education loans become significantly riskier at long durations.

For detailed analysis and code, please refer to the Jupyter notebook.
